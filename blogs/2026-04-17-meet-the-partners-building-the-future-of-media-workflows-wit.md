<p>Modern media workflows don&#8217;t run on storage alone. They run on ecosystems—tightly integrated stacks of tools that ingest, process, analyze, move, review, and <a href="https://www.backblaze.com/cloud-storage/solutions/media-workflow" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">distribute content at scale</a>. The companies we partner with aren&#8217;t just <a href="https://www.backblaze.com/cloud-storage/integrations" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">integrating with Backblaze</a>; they&#8217;re building on it to solve real, specific problems that media teams face every day: archives that are impossible to search, file transfers that collapse over distance, review cycles that slow production to a crawl, compliance workflows that can&#8217;t keep up with library growth.</p>



<p>At NAB Show 2026, we&#8217;re bringing ten of those partners into <a href="https://www.backblaze.com/nab2026" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>booth N1259</strong></a> to share what they&#8217;ve built. But the stories they&#8217;re telling aren&#8217;t just for Las Vegas—they&#8217;re for any media team trying to understand what a modern, cost-efficient, deeply integrated workflow actually looks like in practice.</p>



<p>Here&#8217;s a look at each partner, the problem they solve, and why it matters.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Imaginario AI: From Archive to Action</h2>



<p><strong>Presenter: Jose Puga, CEO and Co-Founder, </strong><a href="https://imaginario.ai/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>Imaginario AI</strong></a></p>



<p>Most video archives are passive. Content sits in storage, cataloged by someone who had time to do it years ago, effectively invisible to the people who could use it today. Imaginario AI flips that model.</p>



<p>Jose Puga&#8217;s session, &#8220;From Archive to Action: How AI Is Turning Your Video Library Into a Revenue Engine,&#8221; frames the shift from passive storage and cataloging to intelligent systems that actively surface, repurpose, and monetize content. The talk covers how marketing teams can accelerate campaigns, post-production teams can shortcut assembly, and compliance workflows can move from reactive to automated.</p>



<p>Imaginario is a multimodal video agentic platform that understands video in a human-like way—recognizing sounds, speech, and visual scenes without requiring time-based metadata. The platform is trusted by Warner Bros. Discovery, Sony Music, and Universal Pictures, and has won back-to-back IBC Best of Show honors in 2024 and 2025.</p>



<p>The Backblaze integration means content stays where it lives—no migration, no friction. As Jose puts it: &#8220;Backblaze provides our clients a scalable, reliable cloud layer that eliminates the friction of manual data transfers. Our native integration saves video teams dozens of hours by keeping content on Backblaze while unlocking high-speed AI analysis, retrieval, and repurposing in media and entertainment.&#8221;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Suite Studios: S3 Native File Streaming</h2>



<p><strong>Presenter: Jay Maxwell, Co-Founder &amp; CPO, </strong><a href="https://www.suitestudios.io/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>Suite Studios</strong></a></p>



<p>Remote and distributed production teams have wrestled with the same problem for years: How do you <a href="https://www.backblaze.com/blog/stack-to-win-a-powerful-solution-for-sports-media-production/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">collaborate on large media files</a> when your team is spread across the globe? Suite&#8217;s answer is S3 Native File Streaming on Backblaze B2. The stack makes working with cloud object storage all-around easier, enabling teams to read and write data as standard objects without copying, syncing, or transferring.</p>



<p>Jay Maxwell&#8217;s session explores how global teams can instantly access and collaborate on large media files from anywhere—eliminating delays and accelerating production timelines. No syncing, no waiting, no workarounds. Just files that behave the way you need them to, wherever your team happens to be.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">TwelveLabs: Unlocking Archive Value with Video AI</h2>



<p><strong>Presenter: Jordan Woods, Senior Solutions Architect, </strong><a href="https://www.twelvelabs.io/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>TwelveLabs</strong></a></p>



<p>There&#8217;s a pattern across media organizations of all sizes: years of valuable archived content sitting effectively invisible because manual cataloging doesn&#8217;t scale. TwelveLabs makes that <a href="https://www.brighttalk.com/webcast/14807/648569" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">content searchable and monetizable</a>.</p>



<p>Jordan Woods&#8217; session, &#8220;Unlocking Archive Value with TwelveLabs Video Intelligence,&#8221; shows how pairing Backblaze&#8217;s cost-efficient storage with TwelveLabs&#8217; video AI gives organizations a practical path to turn dormant archives into searchable, monetizable assets.</p>



<p>As Danny Nicolopoulos, Head of Strategic Partnerships at TwelveLabs, explains: &#8220;Media companies are sitting on years of valuable archived content that&#8217;s effectively invisible because manual cataloging doesn&#8217;t scale. <a href="https://www.backblaze.com/blog/ai-video-understanding-in-your-apps-with-twelve-labs-and-backblaze/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Pairing Backblaze&#8217;s cost-efficient storage with TwelveLabs&#8217; video AI</a> gives organizations a practical path to turn those dormant archives into searchable, monetizable assets.&#8221;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Tillered: Full Speed Cloud Storage Over Any Distance</h2>



<p><strong>Presenters: Davey Goode, Founder &amp; Stephen Ceci, Commercial Lead, </strong><a href="https://www.tillered.io/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>Tillered</strong></a></p>



<p>Physics is an undefeated opponent. <a href="https://www.backblaze.com/blog/network-stats-for-q4-2025-neocloud-traffic-trends/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Move data</a> over long distances and throughput drops—no matter how fast your storage is or how much bandwidth you have. Tillered is built to remove that distance penalty.</p>



<p>The session, &#8220;Backblaze B2 Accelerate with Tillered: Full Speed Cloud Storage Over Any Distance,&#8221; demonstrates how the B2 Accelerate product brings Tillered&#8217;s acceleration technology to Backblaze B2, with transfers up to 34x faster as a result. No simulations—just live transfers with real results, showing what it looks like when enterprises can move data across any distance without adding bandwidth or changing workflows.</p>



<p>Davey Goode founded Tillered after repeatedly running into the same issue building large-scale connectivity infrastructure: No matter how much bandwidth was available, performance collapsed over distance. This session is the solution he built.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Telestream UP.Review: Decide Faster</h2>



<p><strong>Presenter: Sebastian Manemann, Senior Director of Product Management, </strong><a href="https://www.telestream.com/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>Telestream</strong></a></p>



<p>Capture, storage, and distribution have gotten faster. Review hasn&#8217;t kept up. Proxies, downloads, duplicated files—the approval workflow often undoes the speed gains made everywhere else in the pipeline.</p>



<p>Sebastian Manemann&#8217;s session, &#8220;Decide Faster: Real-Time Review, QC, and Delivery with Telestream UP.Review and Backblaze B2,&#8221; demos how Telestream UP.Review enables real-time, high-resolution playback and QC directly on <a href="https://www.backblaze.com/blog/automate-your-digital-media-workflows-with-backblaze-and-telestream/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">media stored in Backblaze B2</a> without moving or duplicating files.</p>



<p>By keeping content in Backblaze as the central storage layer, teams can review instantly, validate quality, and move to delivery faster—all while maintaining a single source of truth across the workflow. As Sebastian frames it: &#8220;When you eliminate duplication and review directly from storage, you don&#8217;t just move faster—you operate smarter.&#8221;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Qencode + Sardius: A Multi-Vendor M&amp;E Workflow in Practice</h2>



<p><strong>Presenters: Murad Mordukhay, CEO, </strong><a href="https://cloud.qencode.com/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>Qencode</strong></a><strong>, and Jason Shore &amp; Ari Burt, </strong><a href="https://sardius.media/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>Sardius Media</strong></a></p>



<p>This one is different from the others—it&#8217;s not a product demo; <a href="https://www.backblaze.com/cloud-storage/case-studies/sardius" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">it&#8217;s a case study</a>. Sardius Media rebuilt their media pipeline around a race model: Multiple storage providers write in parallel, and the fastest result wins.</p>



<p>The session breaks down how Backblaze wins approximately 80% of those races, with Qencode orchestrating the encoding layer across the pipeline. It covers what the production data actually shows, and what Sardius would do differently if they were designing the workflow from scratch today.</p>



<p>Qencode uses machine learning to reduce file sizes by an average of 60% without sacrificing quality. Their CEO, Murad Mordukhay, sums up the partnership plainly: &#8220;The best partnerships are when two companies solve different halves of the same problem. Backblaze handles storage with the kind of reliability and transparency that perfectly aligns with how we think about transcoding. No surprises, no lock-in, just infrastructure that earns trust by performing every single day.&#8221;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">HiScale: Orchestrating Media Across the Stack</h2>



<p><strong>Presenter: Christoph Jurkuhn, CEO, </strong><a href="https://www.hiscale.com/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>HiScale</strong></a></p>

<p>Christoph Jurkuhn&#8217;s session, &#8220;The Hidden Layer: Orchestrating Media Across the Stack with HiScale and Backblaze,&#8221; shows in a live demo how HiScale orchestrates media movement across distributed environments while Backblaze B2 provides the durable, scalable storage layer underneath. Files move, sync, and become available across teams and tools without delays, duplication, or manual intervention.</p>



<p>&#8220;Backblaze is built for straightforward, cost-efficient storage and HiScale builds on top of that to orchestrate and process your media. It&#8217;s a natural fit for <a href="https://www.backblaze.com/contact-sales/passing-the-cloud-tipping-point-why-media-workflows-are-embracing-cloud-storage-on-their-own-terms" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">modern media workflows</a>.&#8221;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Iconik: Control Your Content</h2>



<p><strong>Presenter: Jeremy Strootman, Media Workflow Consultant, </strong><a href="https://www.iconik.io/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong>Iconik</strong></a></p>

<p>Jeremy Strootman&#8217;s session, &#8220;Control Your Content: Turning Media Chaos into Operational Advantage,&#8221; demos <a href="https://www.backblaze.com/docs/cloud-storage-integrate-iconik-with-backblaze-b2" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">how Iconik and Backblaze B2</a> give teams operational control over their media: making content instantly searchable, accessible, and reusable across the entire workflow.</p>



<p>With a foundational background in storage and archiving, Jeremy has spent over two decades helping content creators maximize efficiency and productivity across the entire media lifecycle, from ingest to archive. His perspective: &#8220;Backblaze gives us the scalable foundation; Iconik gives teams control over how that content is actually used.&#8221;</p>

<p>Peter Steiner&#8217;s session, &#8220;Record, Store, Play: Modern Broadcast Workflows on Backblaze,&#8221; brings ToolsOnAir&#8217;s deep broadcast expertise to bear on what it looks like to build a complete ingest-to-playout workflow with Backblaze B2 as the storage foundation.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Mixpeek: AI-Powered Media Compliance at Scale</h2>

<p>The final session tackles a problem that&#8217;s only growing: As video libraries scale into the billions of assets, compliance workflows built for smaller operations don&#8217;t hold.</p>
