Enterprise leaders recognize AI's potential but lack confidence in their infrastructure to support it, with only 22% feeling prepared. Organizations that treat storage as a separate cost concern rather than integrating data strategy with AI strategy tend to see misalignment that leads to abandoned projects. Companies that align data and AI strategy together achieve better outcomes through strategic infrastructure decisions, better-funded initiatives, and shared accountability.
